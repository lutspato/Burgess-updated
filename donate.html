KCB Mobile Banking

Make instant donations using KCB Mobile Banking service

How to Donate via KCB Mobile Banking:

STEP 1

Dial *244*9# on your phone

  • Select 1 to pay Merchant
  • Enter Merchant ID: 124788
  • Enter your Reference Number
  • Enter the Amount

STEP 2

  • Enter payment reason (e.g., "Donation")
  • Select 1 to confirm payment
  • Enter your PIN to confirm
  • Payment Successful

STEP 3

You will receive a confirmation message with your transaction details
