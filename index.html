Welcome to Burgess Foundation

The Burgess Foundation is a nonprofit community-based organization focused on improving the lives of children, youth, and women in Uganda. We create sustainable change through education, health, skills development, and economic empowerment programs.

Our Programs

Support To Education

Education is key in breaking the cycle of generational poverty. Education provides a sense of normalcy, safety and the knowledge needed to build a more stable, peaceful future. We partner with top performing schools to meet the educational needs of children.

Maternal and Child Health

In rural Uganda, basic medical care is often poor the difference between life and death. Poor nutrition, diarrhea, HIV/AIDS, malaria, anemia, and complications during childbirth are a few examples. Burgess Foundation works with health workers to strengthen the public health access alongside community health workers, health volunteers, medical students, and others.

Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH)

61% of Ugandans lack access to safe drinking water. Access to clean water is essential to the health and well-being of every community. Drinking contaminated water often brings illness and death. In order to address clean water challenges in rural communities.
